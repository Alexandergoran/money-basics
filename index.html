Free help that’s actually free

Any kind of help, one call: dial 211 — free, 24/7, they point you to food, housing, and bill help near you. Online: findhelp.org

Free tax filing (VITA): IRS-sponsored, human help, no charge. Many workers miss refunds worth thousands (the EITC). Search “VITA” at irs.gov

Your real credit report, free: annualcreditreport.com — the official one. Ignore lookalike sites that charge.

A bank account that can’t surprise you: bankon.org — accounts with no overdraft and no minimum, even with past banking problems.
