# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Guests of the Hoboken Shelter — adults navigating housing insecurity, often on old low-end Android phones, sometimes with spotty data, sometimes at night. Many are unbanked or fee-burdened; some are Spanish-speaking. They arrive stressed, not curious: the tool must respect their time and dignity.

## Product Purpose

A free, private, single-file money tool: plan a week's budget (steady or irregular pay), see what predatory fees cost per year, set a savings goal, learn credit basics in plain language, and decide which bills to pay first when money is short. Success = someone leaves with one clear number and one next step, and nothing they typed ever leaves the phone.

## Brand Personality

Confident, plain, trustworthy. A serious money app that speaks like a good caseworker: direct, warm, never preachy, never salesy. Editorial sharpness (financial-press energy), not startup energy.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI purple/indigo/violet gradients and glassmorphism ("AI slop")
- Cream-paper + terracotta AI-default editorial look (v0 leaned this way; v1 must not)
- Bank-marketing cheerfulness, stock-photo finance, patronizing "money is fun!" tone
- Crypto/terminal acid-green dark mode

Positive references: YNAB (clarity), Lithic (dark, sleek, professional), Monarch Money, Empower, Rocket Money.

## Design Principles

1. **One number, always visible** — the verdict (left over / short) is the product; it should never be more than a glance away.
2. **Dignity by default** — no shame mechanics, no red alarms for being short; the tool states facts and next moves.
3. **The receipt is the voice** — money math is shown the way money is shown in this world: printed, itemized, on tape.
4. **Works on the worst phone in the room** — every choice is tested against an old low-end Android: no heavy JS, no layout thrash, offline after first load.
5. **Both languages are first-class** — EN/ES parity in every string; Spanish is never a partial translation.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast everywhere (4.5:1 body text), visible keyboard focus, ≥48px touch targets, prefers-reduced-motion respected, prefers-color-scheme respected (deliberate light AND dark), works without JavaScript degraded (noscript notice), zero network calls after load, localStorage only.
